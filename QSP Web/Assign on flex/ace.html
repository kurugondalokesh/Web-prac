Portgas D. Ace

Portgas D. Ace,[12] born as Gol D. Ace[5] and nicknamed "Fire Fist" Ace,[4] was the sworn older brother of Luffy[6] and Sabo,[13] and the biological son of the late Pirate King, Gol D. Roger, and Portgas D. Rouge.[14] Ace was adopted by Monkey D. Garp, as had been requested by Roger before his execution.[15] Ace was the 2nd division commander of the Whitebeard Pirates[2] and one-time captain of the Spade Pirates.
