Red Haired Shanks

Shanks (シャンクス Shankusu), also known as "Red-Haired Shanks" (赤髪のシャンクス Akagami no Shankusu), is a major character of the One Piece franchise. He is a legendary and powerful pirate in the Grand Line, being the chief of the Red Haired Pirates and one of the Four Current Emperors residing at the second half of the Grand Line. With a standing bounty of 4,048,900,000 berries, after the downfall of Big Mom and Kaido, he is now the Pirate with the highest bounty, and has the 5th Highest Bounty known in history. He is also the adoptive father of the World Greatest Diva Uta.
